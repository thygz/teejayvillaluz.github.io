Why Choose Us?



Traxxjester Auto Service is a group of local auto repair shops based in Cavite Philippines. We provide distinctive auto repair and service for businesses and individuals with old fashioned service, stellar customer care, and modern technology.

Our services for both foreign and domestic vehicles include brake inspections, electrical and battery checks, suspensions and alignment work, and more. We offer affordable pricing with no hidden fees - our pricing is always upfront. We also give free phone estimates and welcome walk-ins.
